### **Project 1: Building and Training a Custom Neural Network on Synthetic and MNIST Data**

**Project Summary:**  


<p class="summary-text">
    This project focuses on implementing a custom neural network from scratch using Python and NumPy, emphasizing foundational understanding and practical application. The network was trained and evaluated on synthetic data and the MNIST dataset, employing techniques such as forward and backward propagation, gradient descent, and mini-batch training. Performance analysis over five epochs demonstrated effective learning and strong generalization, with validation loss consistently lower than training loss. To optimize the model's performance, weights initialization and learning rate evaluations were conducted, identifying hyperparameters that ensured stable convergence and effective learning. The model achieved a 95% accuracy on the MNIST test dataset, with evaluation metrics such as a confusion matrix confirming its accuracy and stability. The project showcases essential deep learning principles while highlighting optimization techniques for real-world applications.
</p>
